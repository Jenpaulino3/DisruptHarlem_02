What Is Optical.ly?

Optical.ly is a mobile responsive app that provides an online platform
for traditional/brick and mortar optical stores.
Customers can schedule appointments, receive virtual assistance and access other services.
Business owners can track thir customers, submit featured content, and increase the customer experience

Get Started!

What We Provide

Appointments

Schedule or manage an eye exam for eyeglasses/contact lenses or emergency visits. Reminder options available

Services

Information on what to expect during your visit, FAQs, shopping guide, exclusive offers, and virtual assistance

Exclusivity

Our services are only available to subscribers and we offer exclusive deals for downloading our app

Two-Sided Dashboard

Customers can track rewards and view location content
Businesses can track subscribers stats and submit content
